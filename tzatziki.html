Tzatziki

This recipe uses mint (which means it's Turkish), but you can substitute dill (to make it Greek). It has quite a bite of raw garlic, so scale back on the number of cloves if you're not into that.

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • salt

Instructions

  • Cut the cucumber in half and scoop out the seeds.
  • Grate the cucumber and put it in a strainer. Sprinkle a little salt over it and mix it in. Let it drain for 5-10 minutes, then squeeze it down to get out as much juice as possible.
  • Mix the cucumber with the rest of the ingredients.

Yield: about 4 cups

Prep time: 00:10

Cook time: 00:20
